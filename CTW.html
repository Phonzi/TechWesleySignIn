Come, let us worship and bow down; let us kneel before Christ, our Redeemer—

 Who was anointed by the spirit of God to bring good news to the poor.

 Come, let us worship and bow down; let us kneel before Christ, our Redeemer—

 Who was crucified by the keepers of order for heeding the summons of God.

 Come, let us worship and bow down; let us kneel before Christ, our Redeemer—

 Who baptizes us with the Holy Spirit for bringing the reign of God to earth.

 Come, let us worship and bow down; let us kneel before Christ, our Redeemer!
